The 411

New to the Harbour City? Whether you are just visiting for the weekend or planning to put down roots, here are the essential facts, figures, and logistics you need to navigate Nanaimo.

Fast Facts

Population Approx. 100,000 (Central Nanaimo)
Nickname The Harbour City
Known For Nanaimo Bars, Bathtub Racing, The Bastion
Climate Mediterranean (Dry summers, mild wet winters)
Ferry Terminals Departure Bay (to Horseshoe Bay) & Duke Point (to Tsawwassen)

Getting Around

BC Ferries: The lifeline of the island. Reservations are highly recommended for weekends and holidays. Foot passengers can travel easily between Departure Bay and Horseshoe Bay.

Seaplanes: Harbour Air and Seair offer 20-minute flights from downtown Nanaimo directly to downtown Vancouver.

Transit: The RDN Transit system services the entire region, including connectors to Parksville and Qualicum Beach.
